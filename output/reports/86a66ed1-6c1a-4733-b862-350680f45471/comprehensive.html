1. Business Overview

EWM Global is a 24-year-old fintech services company specializing in deferred compensation plan administration, generating $24M in annual revenue with 170 employees across three locations. The company serves the financial services industry with three service lines: Deferred Cash Plan Administration (69% of revenue), Carry/Coinvestment Administration (30%), and Share Plan Administration (1%).

Current Market Position: EWM Global has built an enviable customer franchise with industry-leading retention (98%) and lifetime value ($1M per customer). However, this exceptional customer foundation sits atop an increasingly fragile operational and financial structure that threatens both growth ambitions and business continuity.

The Central Paradox: EWM Global is a company that succeeds despite itself—achieving world-class customer outcomes through unsustainable effort intensity rather than systematic excellence. This model has reached its structural limits.

2. Key Findings

Finding 1: Exceptional Customer Economics Are Underleveraged

EWM Global possesses customer metrics that rank among the best in the industry:

  • $1,000,000 customer lifetime value (154x industry median)
  • 98% customer retention rate (2.5x industry median)
  • 18.5:1 LTV:CAC ratio (3x "healthy" threshold)
  • $70,000 average deal size (8.75x industry median)

The Gap: Despite these assets, the company has no structured expansion revenue program. Estimated annual expansion potential of $4.4M remains largely untapped, representing the fastest path to growth with lowest risk.
